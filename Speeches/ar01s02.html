The biggest problems

The biggest problem with GNOME and translation today is the way that we release packages. It may be a good idea to release often to get bugsfixes out, but it is important that the quality of translation of the released package doesn't decrease at the same time. Often it is enough to give the translators a week to update the translations, but unfortunately not always.

There are very few translators for each language and it is an enormous workload to keep all the translations up to date. Therefore a week is not always sufficient; and what do we do when translators are ill, on vacation and so on?

There are many more issues than these, but I have tried to come up with a solution to some of them by making a little set of guidelines for releasing.
